Boston Outside Film Festival

The Boston Outdoor Film Festival (BOFF), is an International, competitive festival. BOFF mission is to provide a public forum in Boston in order to advance public interest in films and the independent production of films. To draw worldwide attention to Boston as a center for cinema. To encourage the rights of all Boston residents to access and experience the power of independent filmmaking, and to promote artistic excellence and the creative freedom of artists without censure. Watch It Outside Boston, is a not-for-profit organization.

Ratatouille (2007)

  • Drama, Thriller
  • 1h 50min
5th Aug - 10 p.m / 7th Aug - 10 p.m / 8th Aug - 8 p.m
Overview

A rat named Remy dreams of becoming a great French chef despite his family's wishes and the obvious problem of being a rat in a decidedly rodent-phobic profession. When fate places Remy in the sewers of Paris, he finds himself ideally situated beneath a restaurant made famous by his culinary hero, Auguste Gusteau. Despite the apparent dangers of being an unlikely - and certainly unwanted - visitor in the kitchen of a fine French restaurant, Remy's passion for cooking soon sets into motion a hilarious and exciting rat race that turns the culinary world of Paris upside down.
